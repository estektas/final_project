Frequently Asked Questions

Whether you are new to Fire Emblem: Three Houses or returning to revisit the Blue Lions route, you may have questions about the game, its characters, or the purpose of this website. This page provides answers to some of the most commonly asked questions to help you better understand the world of Fódlan and navigate the archive. If your question is not answered here, feel free to continue exploring the website for more detailed information on the story, characters, and lore.

  • What is Fire Emblem: Three Houses?

    Fire Emblem: Three Houses is a tactical role-playing game developed by Intelligent Systems and published by Nintendo for the Nintendo Switch. Players take on the role of Byleth, a professor at Garreg Mach Monastery, where they choose to lead one of three student houses. Throughout the game, players build relationships with students, participate in strategic battles, and shape the story through their decisions.

  • Do I need to play previous Fire Emblem games first?

    No. Fire Emblem: Three Houses tells its own self-contained story, making it an excellent entry point for newcomers to the series. While longtime fans may recognize recurring themes and gameplay mechanics, no prior knowledge of previous Fire Emblem titles is required.

  • Why does this website focus on the Blue Lions route?

    This website is dedicated to the Blue Lions route because it offers an in-depth exploration of the Kingdom of Faerghus, Prince Dimitri, and the personal journeys of the Blue Lions students. Focusing on a single route allows the website to provide more detailed character profiles, story summaries, and lore while keeping the information organized and easy to navigate.

  • Who is the leader of the Blue Lions?

    The Blue Lions are led by Dimitri Alexandre Blaiddyd, the crown prince of the Holy Kingdom of Faerghus. As the house leader, Dimitri guides his classmates through their studies and battles at Garreg Mach Monastery. Throughout the Blue Lions route, the player assumes the role of Byleth, the house's professor, whose guidance and mentorship play a significant role in Dimitri's personal growth and leadership. Together, Byleth and Dimitri help shape the future of Faerghus while confronting the challenges that arise throughout the story.

  • Which route should I play?

    There is no single correct route to begin with, as each provides a different perspective on the world of Fódlan. Many players recommend choosing the house whose characters or storyline interest you the most. This website focuses specifically on the Blue Lions route for players who are interested in learning more about its character and story.

  • Can I recruit characters from other houses?

    Yes. Most students from other houses can be recruited by meeting certain skill and support requirements during the academy phase of the game. However, a few characters like Edelgard, Claude, Hubert, and Jeritza are exclusive to their perspective routes and cannot be recruited. You can find more information about their recruitment requirements on SerenesForest – Recruitment.

  • Will this website contain spoilers?

    Yes. Because this website serves as an archive of the Blue Lions route, it includes story summaries, character development, and major plot events. Spoiler warnings will be provided where appropriate, and some sections may use collapsible spoiler tags so visitors can choose whether to reveal story details.

  • Where can I purchase the game?

    This game can be purchased from Nintendo. You have the option of purchasing it in three different variations: Digital, Digital + Expansion Pass, and Physical. Both the Digital and the Physical versions of the game retail for about $59.99 while the Digital + Expansion Pass runs about $84.98.
