Mercedes von Martritz

“It’s so nice to meet you! My name is Mercedes von Martritz, but you can just call me Mercie.” - Mercedes, Fire Emblem Heroes

  • Title: Former Noble of House Martritz
  • Affiliation: Holy Kingdom of Faerghus, Garreg Mach Monastery, Church of Seiros
  • Role: Member of the Blue Lions House

Biography Mercedes von Martritz is a student of the Blue Lions house at Garreg Mach Monastery and a devoted follower of the Church of Seiros. Born into the noble House Martritz of the Adrestian Empire, Mercedes was forced to leave her homeland after a series of family tragedies and eventually settled in the Holy Kingdom of Faerghus with her mother. Hoping to one day become a priest, she enrolled at the Officers Academy to further her education while continuing to live according to her compassionate beliefs. Her past experiences with nobility and the Crest system have given her a unique perspective on kindness, forgiveness, and the value of family.

Personality Mercedes is gentle, patient, and exceptionally caring, often acting as the older sister of the Blue Lions. She is quick to comfort others and approaches every situation with empathy rather than judgment. While her soft-spoken nature may make her appear timid, Mercedes possesses quiet confidence and remarkable emotional strength. Her optimism, faith, and willingness to forgive others allow her to remain a source of comfort even during difficult times.

Role in the Blue Lions Route Throughout the Blue Lions route, Mercedes serves as a compassionate voice among her classmates, offering encouragement and emotional support when tensions arise. Her story explores themes of faith, family, forgiveness, and healing, reminding those around her that strength can take many forms. As the Blue Lions face the hardships of war, Mercedes’ kindness helps keep the group united while demonstrating that compassion can be just as powerful as courage on the battlefield.

Relationships Mercedes develops close friendships with every member of the Blue Lions, often providing guidance and encouragement whenever they need it most. Her nurturing personality allows her to connect easily with students from every house, and her support conversations reveal both her cheerful outlook and the hardships she has overcome. Her relationship with Annette is especially close, with the two often studying together and supporting one another throughout their time at the monastery.

Fun Facts

  • Mercedes bears the Major Crest of Lamine, one of the Crests tied to Fódlan’s noble families.
  • She hopes to become a priest after completing her studies at Garreg Mach Monastery.
  • Mercedes enjoys baking sweets, sewing, and caring for others whenever she can.
  • Although she often appears calm and graceful, Mercedes has a playful sense of humor and occasionally surprises her classmates with unexpected jokes and teasing.
